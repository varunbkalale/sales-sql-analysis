# 📊 Sales Performance Analysis using SQL

## 📌 Objective
The goal of this project is to analyze sales performance across regions and products, compare actual revenue against budget targets, and identify key drivers of profitability.

---

## 📊 Dataset Description
The dataset contains the following fields:

- Date  
- Region  
- Product  
- Customer  
- Revenue  
- Expense  
- Budget_Revenue  
- Profit  

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed

- Total Revenue and Profit calculation  
- Revenue and Profit by Region  
- Top-performing Products by Revenue  
- Top Customers by Revenue  
- Revenue vs Budget comparison  
- Profit Margin analysis by Product  
- Revenue contribution by Region  

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Laptop products contribute the largest share of total revenue (~45.66%)  
- Desktop products have the highest profit margin (~31.85%)  
- Bangalore region generates the highest total profit  
- Hyderabad shows relatively lower performance  
- Revenue is concentrated among a small number of products, indicating potential business risk  

---

## 📈 Business Recommendations

- Improve profit margins for high-revenue products like laptops  
- Focus on expanding high-margin product categories  
- Improve performance in underperforming regions  
- Diversify product portfolio to reduce dependency risk
